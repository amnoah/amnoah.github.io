Leaflet marker. These forks exist to provide additional colors of the marker.

properly attribute it. As such, we are crediting it here, in our code, and on the final website. We have also added
comments inside each of the photos (since at its most basic level a svg image is a xml file) containing the original
license as to reproduce the license in the binary.
